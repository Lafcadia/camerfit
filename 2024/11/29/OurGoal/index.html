Market Stage and Growth Trends

For Action Cameras:

The action camera market is growing, powered by increased adoption for adventure sports, content creation (e.g., vlogging), and social media trends.
In the short-term: its growth is fueled by social media’s impact, where users seek high-quality, portable cameras for dynamic content. For the long-term: Expansion into new markets like augmented reality (AR) integration, enhanced stabilization, and immersive content formats (e.g., 360-degree cameras).The Key Players are GoPro dominates, followed by DJI, Insta360, and Akaso. These brands can be cooperation targets.

With the trend of increasing market of Action Camera and contracting market of Digital Camera, the basic auxiliary devices market seems to become competitive, while the advanced area with diverse usage requires technological development, should be the focus of our products. The competitors are Ulanzi、Joby and so on.
